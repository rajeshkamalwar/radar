# RADAR

**R.A.D.A.R.** means **Reputation, Authority, Discovery, Analytics & Response**.

RADAR is an enterprise brand visibility intelligence OS. It monitors, predicts, improves, and protects how a brand is found, ranked, cited, reviewed, discussed, trusted, and chosen across search engines, AI platforms, social media, reviews, influencers, media, marketplaces, and local discovery surfaces.

## Positioning

RADAR is not one mixed dashboard. It is a command system made of specialist intelligence centers. Each center has its own data, authority, workflows, scoring, automation, and governance. The Executive RADAR layer connects them into one leadership view.

## Core RADAR Systems

- Search RADAR
- AI RADAR
- Reputation RADAR
- Review RADAR
- Local RADAR
- Social RADAR
- Influencer RADAR
- Trend RADAR
- Authority RADAR
- Competitor RADAR
- Funnel RADAR
- Executive RADAR

## North Star

Make every public signal about a brand measurable, explainable, predictable, and actionable.
